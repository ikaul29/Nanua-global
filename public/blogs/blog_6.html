How to learn English quickly?

English is one of the top 10 most spoken languages in the world. It is a fun language to learn not only for its words, phrases , idioms , spellings….but also because simply changing the tone of a particular sentence can add a totally different meaning to it. Its intricacies & complexities can easily perplex the most diligent learner. And learning English fast might seem next to impossible.

But it’s NOT! As long as you have the RIGHT strategy.

Here are the 8 Golden tips: 1) Read more! Make reading a habit. Try reading anything & everything you can get your hands on. Be it newspapers, emails, novels, websites etc. All this material is full of a lot of words as well as fair amount of words that you already know. Learning new words play an important role in building your vocabulary.

2) Make notes Always pen down new words & phrases. It is a tried & tested way to improve your English prowess. While reading or writing whenever you come across a new word, make a habit of jotting it down. This has two benefits. First it will improve the chances of remembering the meaning. And second even if you forget it , it saves you a lot of time and you don't have to search for the meaning again.

3) Practice practice practice The best way to learn any language is to start communicating in that language this will help you learn a lot faster than just reading/writing in English. Talk to your friends & family in English and ask them to correct your mistakes.

4) Don't be scared of mistakes Even native English speakers make mistakes. And it's nothing to be scared of. Your ultimate aim should be to be able to convey your thoughts/message, not speak like an English language expert. Infact mistakes are the best way to learn, improve and move forward.

5) Practice listening There are web series, movies, podcasts, YouTube channels in English covering all genres. Subscribe to or follow the ones that matches with your interests. Watch and listen to these in your breaks and leisure time. At first, you might find the native accents difficult to understand, but be patient and soon you'll be comfortable with it and rather start enjoying it!

6) Set small, achievable & realistic goals Setting weekly & monthly goals play a crucial role in accessing your progress. If you are able to achieve your targets in time then you should be proud of your progress. And if not then you should pull up your socks and work harder.

7) Try Shadowing technique It's an amazing language learning technique wherein you repeat the audio right after listening to it. This helps improve your pronunciation, diction and boosts your confidence. This technique should be practiced daily for 15 minutes to see a significant improvement.
